About Lottery Simulator

Lottery Simulator is simple web application designed to demostrate chances for winning the lottery. It simulates lottery drawing day by day, week by week and comparing randomly generated (drawed) numbers with user selected numbers. Simulator was written by enthusiastic developer who also enjoy playing lottery.

If you manage to win jackpot in lottery simulator, maybe you'l get lucky in real life!
